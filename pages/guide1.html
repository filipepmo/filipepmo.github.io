Goal of this Document

Create a boilerplate document outlining which strategies can be used in different phases of a team's development with a view of creating an effective team that celebrates the values of excellence, being a team player and respect.

Motivation

I had both great managers and terrible ones. I've been both on great and terrible teams. Probably just as you did.

While it's easy to pinpoint what makes teams and managers terrible, it is hard to identify what works well consistently over time and when to recognize and adapt to change.

It is my firm belief that the development of people is key to a long lasting success of the company and should be the primary concern of management.

You need feedback to learn and become an expert. So creating a learning organization is fundamental to achieve this.

Software development is not a fixed process where everytime you repeat a combination of steps you achieve the same outcome. Instead it is based on experimentation and follows a scientific pattern to problem-solving.

Also having fun while working is important for a long-lasting team and leading a happy life.
